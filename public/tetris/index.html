About this game

Play a classic falling-block puzzle online

This free browser game includes the seven classic tetrominoes, separate classic and power-up modes, increasing gravity, line-clear scoring, and local high scores.

Keyboard and touch controls

Play with arrow keys on desktop or use the six large touch buttons on phones and tablets.

Classic or tactical play

Keep the original rules or earn one energy for every four natural line clears and spend it on three rescue tools.

Optional browser audio

Enable synthesized background music and sound effects when you want them. Audio starts muted by default.
