About this game

Play a classic falling-block puzzle online

This free browser game includes the seven classic tetrominoes, ghost-piece placement, increasing gravity, line-clear scoring, and a high score saved only on this device.

Keyboard and touch controls

Play with arrow keys on desktop or use the six large touch buttons on phones and tablets.

Classic level progression

Advance one level for every ten cleared lines as gravity accelerates from one second to a 100 millisecond minimum.

Optional browser audio

Enable synthesized background music and sound effects when you want them. Audio starts muted by default.
